Implementation Details​

Government of British Columbia​

  • UNTP commitment made on: 11-Sept-2024
  • Registration Country: Canada
  • Operting Countries: Canada
  • Participate in pre-release UNTP Pilots? : Yes
LogoImplementation Statement
The BC government sees UNTP implementation as an opportunity to enable BC producers of raw materials to differentiate their products in emerging sustainability-focused markets, contributing to a sustainable, clean, secure, and fair global economy.
